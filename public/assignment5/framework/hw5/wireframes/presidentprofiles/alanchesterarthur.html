Presidency Details: Arthur was nominated by the Republicans for vice president. He got the nomination primarily because of political reasons and as a favor to Arthur's powerful friend, Senator Roscoe Conkling. Arthur had never before held an elected office. His lack of experience made some people nervous, and they hoped that nothing would happen to President Garfield.
