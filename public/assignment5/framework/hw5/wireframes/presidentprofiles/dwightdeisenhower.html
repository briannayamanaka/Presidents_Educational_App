Dwight D. Eisenhower

Presidency Details:

Eisenhower was very popular and easily won the 1952 presidential election. Eisenhower's two presidential terms were a time of economic prosperity and relative peace. Some of his accomplishments include the Eisenhower Doctrine, the Interstate Highway System, the Civil Rights Acts, and an end to the Korean War.
